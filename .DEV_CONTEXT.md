## 🎨 Design Decisions

- **Theme**: Cyberpunk dark (deep purple #8B5CF6 + cyan #06B6D4)
- **Font**: Inter (UI) + JetBrains Mono (code)
- **State**: Provider (simple, well-known)
- **Storage**: SQLite (sqflite) + SharedPreferences + Hive + FlutterSecureStorage
- **Architecture**: Singleton services with ChangeNotifier
- **Naming**: DroidClaw (Android + OpenClaw)
